Use your referral link to invite people to MemePerfect. You earn a reward only after a referral is verified as qualified.

<Info>
  Reward rates, payout timing, and qualification criteria can change. Always check your referral dashboard for the current terms.
</Info>

## Before you start

Make sure you can access:

- Your referral dashboard
- Your unique referral link
- Your payout settings

## Referral process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Copy your referral link">
    Open your referral dashboard and copy your personal referral URL.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share your link">
    Share your link where your audience already follows you, such as X, Telegram, Discord, YouTube, or your newsletter.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Referral signs up">
    The referral must start signup from your link and complete there subscription payment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="System verifies referral">
    After validation checks pass, the reward is added to your referral balance.
  </Step>
</Steps>


## Payouts

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="When do payouts happen?">
    Payouts follow the schedule shown in your referral dashboard but usually 7 days after payment and total payout must be above $100.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How are payouts sent?">
    Payouts are sent to the destination usdc solana wallet address saved in your payout settings.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why can a referral be removed?">
    A referral can be removed if it is duplicate, self-referred, refunded, or flagged during abuse review.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>


## Track referral performance

Use your referral dashboard to monitor:

- Referral link clicks
- Qualified signups
- Conversion rate
- Pending rewards
- Paid rewards

## Need help?

If tracking or payout data looks wrong, contact support and include:

- Your account email
- Referral link used
- Referred user email (if available)
- Approximate signup date and time
