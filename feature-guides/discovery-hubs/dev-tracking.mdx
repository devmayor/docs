Dev Tracking helps you monitor developers whose launches you want to follow. Use it when you care more about who launched the token than about broad market discovery.

## How Dev Tracking works

1. Start from a token or wallet you want to investigate.
2. Review the developer's history and related wallets.
3. Follow the developer if you want future launches to matter.
4. Use that follow list inside a Strategy.

## Dev Network Explorer

The **Dev Network Explorer** helps you inspect a developer's history.

Use it to review:

- historical success rate
- common launch patterns
- wallet relationships and associated accounts

This gives you context before deciding whether to follow the developer.

## Follow a developer

Click `[+ Follow Dev]` to add to your private lists. This unlocks:

- A Followed Dev Deploys a New Token

You're building a proprietary alpha feed that no one else has.
