When a token passes all your Dealbreakers and Rules, MemePerfect sends you an alert. Here's what you get and what to do next.

### Where alerts appear

**Telegram** — Alerts arrive in the MemePerfect bot. Click the token address to copy it, or use the links to open Axiom, BonkBot, Dextools, and other tools with the token prefilled.

**In-app Alerts page** — Your full alert history. Click any alert for complete details: snapshot at alert time, live performance since alert, which rules passed, and quick actions.

### What's in an alert

Each alert includes:

- **Token** — Name, symbol, logo, contract address
- **Strategy** — Which strategy fired (click to open it)
- **Trigger** — The event type (e.g., Newly Created Tokens, Tweet Mentions Token)
- **Snapshot at alert** — Market cap, liquidity, holders, taxes, price when the alert fired
- **Live performance** — Current market cap, peak market cap, ROI since alert, and a price sparkline

The snapshot tells you what the token looked like when it qualified. The live performance tells you what happened after. Use both to decide whether to act and to refine your strategy.


<Info>
MemePerfect does not execute trades. Alerts give you the token and links. You decide when and how to act.
</Info>

### Linking Telegram

To receive alerts in Telegram, link your account in **Settings**. Start the MemePerfect bot, complete the flow, and alerts will appear there. You can still view everything in the app.

<Tip>
Use the in-app Alerts page to review performance. The live ROI and sparkline help you see which strategies and rules produce winners.
</Tip>
