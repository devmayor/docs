---
title: "Glossary of Terms"
description: "Common crypto and platform terms used throughout MemeTrader."
---

* **Alpha**: An edge or information advantage.
* **Dealbreaker**: Mandatory rule; if failed, evaluation stops immediately.
* **Degen**: High‑risk, high‑reward trader, often memecoin‑focused.
* **FDV (Fully Diluted Valuation)**: Market cap if all tokens were in circulation.
* **Honeypot**: Contract lets you buy but not sell.
* **Liquidity Pool (LP)**: Assets locked in a DEX pair enabling swaps.
* **Rug Pull**: Dev withdraws liquidity, collapsing token value.
* **Slippage**: Difference between expected and executed price.
* **Sniping**: Buying within seconds/minutes of launch for best entry.
