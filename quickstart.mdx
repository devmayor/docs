---
title: "Quick Start: 5 Minutes to Your First Alert"
description: "Sign in with Telegram, clone a template, and activate your first Strategy."
---

This guide helps you get your first Strategy live as quickly as possible. By the end, you will be signed in, running a template, and ready to receive alerts in Telegram.

![MemePerfect start template](/images/memeperfect-start-template.png)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in with Telegram">
    Your MemePerfect account is created when you sign in with Telegram.

    1. Open `https://app.memeperfect.io`.
    2. Click `Continue with Telegram`.

       <img
         src="/images/telegram-login.png"
         alt="Telegram login page"
         title="Telegram login page"
         className="mx-auto"
       />

    3. Telegram opens on your device and starts the MemePerfect bot.
    4. In Telegram, open the login link sent by the bot.
    5. The app loads and your dashboard becomes available.

    ![Telegram login link](/images/loginlink.jpg)

    <Check>
      You are ready for the next step when the dashboard opens successfully.
    </Check>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Clone a template Strategy">
    Start with a template so you can learn the product using working logic instead of a blank page.

    1. Click `+ Create New Strategy`.
    2. Select the `Fort Knox (Max Safety)` template.

       <img
         src="/images/select-template.png"
         alt="Template selection screen"
         title="Template selection"
         style={{ width: "51%" }}
       />

    3. Rename the strategy name to another name you want (e.g., "Fort Knox") 

    4. Click `Next: Trigger` You can add additional triggers if you like

    6. Click `Next: Rules`  

       <img
         src="/images/rules.png"
         alt="Rules Pn"
         title="Rules Pn"
         style={{ width:"58%" }}
       />

    6. Review the preset Rules. Keep the defaults if you want the safest starting point.
    7. On the review step, enable the activation toggle and save the Strategy.

    5. On the Strategies reviews page, click the toggle to activate the strategy

       ![Pasted Graphic 6.png](/images/PastedGraphic6.png)

    <Info>
      This template includes core Dealbreakers such as honeypot, tax, and mint checks.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Receive your first alerts">
    1. Open Telegram.
    2. Wait for tokens that match your Strategy to appear in the MemePerfect bot.

       <img
         src="/images/Takeactionbelow.png"
         alt="Telegram alert example"
         title="Telegram alert"
         style={{ width: "61%" }}
       />

    3. Copy the contract address from the alert if you want to inspect or trade the token elsewhere.
    4. Or use the included links to open tools such as Axiom, Nova, BonkBot, or Dextools.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## You are live

Your Strategy is now active and evaluating new opportunities automatically.
