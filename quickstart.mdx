This guide is for traders who want value now. You'll connect, activate a battle‑tested strategy, and receive alerts in Telegram.

<Frame caption="60‑second overview">
  <img src="/images/hero-light.png" alt="Quick start workflow: connect, clone, link" />
</Frame>

<Steps>
<Step title="Connect your wallet">
  Your account is created by signing a message. We never request private keys or approvals.

  1. Open the app: `https://app.memetrader.ai`
  2. Click `Connect Wallet` (top‑right)
  3. Choose your provider (Phantom, MetaMask)
  4. Sign the login message

  <Check>Dashboard is active after signing.</Check>
</Step>

<Step title="Clone a Safe Launch strategy">
  Start with a template designed to filter scams and low‑effort launches.

  1. Click `+ Create New Strategy`
  2. Select template: "Fort Knox (Max Safety)"
  3. Name it (e.g., "My First Sniper") and `Save Strategy`
  4. On the Strategies page, click `Activate`

  <Info>
  Template includes essential Dealbreakers (honeypot, taxes, mint authority).
  </Info>
</Step>

<Step title="Link Telegram for alerts">
  1. Go to `Settings → Notification Channels`
  2. Click `Generate Link Code` (6‑digit)
  3. In Telegram, open `@MemeTraderBot`
  4. Send: `/link 123-456` (replace with your code)
  5. Wait for confirmation; dashboard shows Connected

  <Tip>
  If the bot doesn't respond within 10s, resend once—Telegram rate‑limits occasionally.
  </Tip>
</Step>
</Steps>

### You're ready

Your strategy is live and monitoring. You'll get actionable alerts for legitimate, high‑potential launches.
