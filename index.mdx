## Why MemePerfect exists

Memecoin markets move fast. Most signals are noise, and manual monitoring does not scale.

MemePerfect helps you turn your market view into an automated alert system. Instead of watching every launch, you define what matters and let the platform monitor it for you.

MemePerfect is a comprehensive, automated alpha platform. It handles the 24/7 scanning, deep on‑chain analysis, and social monitoring so you can focus on high‑conviction decisions.

MemePerfect tracks on-chain activity and social signals 24/7. It evaluates events using your Strategy and sends an alert when your conditions are met.

**Trigger (when something happens) + Rules (what must be true) = Alert**

- A **Trigger** starts evaluation.
- **Rules** decide whether the token qualifies.
- If the token passes, you receive an alert in Telegram with key data and trading links.
